BACKGROUND


'kitahantar' is a service of grocery delivery that deliver fresh products straight to our customers' houses. We have been operating for three years and instead of just selling wet kitchen items, we now also sell favorites of southeast asians vegetables. 'kitahantar' was started by four best friends in 2019 which originally aimed to help facilitate single mothers in Malaysia to buy kitchen items. Starting small at the home of one of these four best friends, unexpectedly it received a very lively response, not only single mothers but we expanded our target audience to bachelors, couples and large families. By using the products and services we provide it makes the daily cooking process easier and more fun. Until now, we are able to serve more than 5 thousand families in Malaysia. With the motto, no sweat, no stress, on time, we guarantee all customers do not have to sweat and rush to buy kitchen items, do not have to stress facing the road, and customers can cook on time whenever they want with full of love and joy.
